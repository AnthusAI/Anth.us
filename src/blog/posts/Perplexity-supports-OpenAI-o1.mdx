Perplexity has [announced](https://x.com/AravSrinivas/status/1835874778768625683) the introduction of a new "Reasoning" focus feature, powered by OpenAI's o1 preview model. This new addition marks a significant step forward in leveraging OpenAI's latest advancements in AI reasoning capabilities.

The new Reasoning focus, utilizing the o1 model, brings sophisticated problem-solving skills to Perplexity. Users can expect more precise and reliable answers to complex queries, benefiting from the model's advanced reasoning capabilities and improved accuracy. Perplexity has seamlessly integrated this new feature into their platform, ensuring a smooth user experience. Currently, Pro customers are allocated 10 uses of the Reasoning focus with o1 per day.

This update highlights Perplexity's commitment to providing specialized tools for specific tasks. The o1 model's advanced reasoning capabilities make the new Reasoning focus particularly well-suited for complex problem-solving, while Perplexity's platform provides an accessible interface for users to leverage these capabilities effectively.

As AI technology continues to evolve, innovations like this demonstrate the potential for combining cutting-edge models with user-friendly platforms to deliver powerful, practical solutions. Perplexity's introduction of the Reasoning focus powered by the OpenAI o1 preview model exemplifies how advanced AI can be made accessible and useful for a broader audience.
